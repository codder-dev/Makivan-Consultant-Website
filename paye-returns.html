Overview

What is PAYE Returns Filing?

PAYE (Pay As You Earn) returns filing is a monthly obligation for employers in Kenya. It involves calculating employee income tax based on salaries, filing the returns with KRA, and remitting the tax by the 9th of every month.

Is PAYE Returns Filing right for you?

FAQ

Common questions about PAYE returns.

When is the PAYE returns deadline?

PAYE returns must be filed by the 9th of every month. If the 9th falls on a weekend or public holiday, the deadline is extended to the next working day.

What happens if I file late?

Late filing attracts penalties and interest charges from KRA. We help ensure timely filing to avoid these costs.

Do I need to file if I have no employees?

If you have no employees, you may still need to file a nil return. We can guide you on your obligations.

What are employee tax certificates?

Employee tax certificates (P9 forms) show how much tax an employee has paid during the year and are required for their personal tax returns.

Can you help with past PAYE issues?

Yes, we can help review past returns, correct errors and negotiate penalty waivers with KRA where applicable.
