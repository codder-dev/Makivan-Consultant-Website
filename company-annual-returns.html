FAQ

Annual returns questions, answered.

When are annual returns due?

Annual returns are typically due within a specific timeframe after the company's incorporation anniversary. We can help you track and meet these deadlines.

What happens if I miss the deadline?

Late filing can result in penalties and may affect your company's compliance status. We help ensure timely filing to avoid these issues.

Do you help with director changes?

Yes. We process director additions, removals and updates to company records as part of our compliance services.

What is beneficial ownership?

Beneficial ownership refers to individuals who ultimately own or control a company. We help process and update these records as required by law.

How much does annual returns filing cost?

Costs vary depending on the complexity of your company structure. Contact us for a personalized quote.
