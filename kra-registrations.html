What are KRA Registrations?

KRA registrations involve obtaining and updating your tax registration documents including PIN certificates, corporate documents and contact information. These are essential for tax compliance and business operations in Kenya.

FAQ

Common questions about KRA registrations.

How do I get a KRA PIN?

You can apply for a KRA PIN online through the iTax system. We can help you with the application process.

What is a CR12 certificate?

CR12 is a certificate from the Registrar of Companies showing the directors and shareholders of a company.

How do I update my KRA contact details?

You can update your contact details through the iTax system. We can help you make these updates correctly.

What documents do I need for PIN registration?

You need your ID/passport, passport photo, contact details and for businesses, company registration documents.

Can you help with CR documents?

Yes, we help you obtain CR12, CR13, CR7 and other corporate documents from KRA.
