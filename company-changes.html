Overview

What are Company Changes?

Company changes refer to updates made to your company's registered details including company linking, address changes, and filings for churches, societies and clubs. These changes must be properly documented and filed with the Registrar of Companies.

FAQ

Common questions about company changes.

What is company linking?

Company linking is the process of establishing relationships between multiple companies that share common directors or shareholders.

Why do I need to update my company address?

You must update your company address to ensure official correspondence reaches you and to maintain compliance with the Registrar of Companies.

Do churches and societies need to file returns?

Yes, churches, societies, clubs and other membership organizations must file annual returns and maintain compliance with the Registrar.

How long does a company change take?

The timeline varies depending on the type of change. We ensure all changes are processed as quickly as possible.

Can you help with past changes?

Yes, we can help regularize past company changes that were not properly documented or filed.
