FAQ

Common questions about business loans.

How do I know if I'm ready for a loan?

We help you assess your loan readiness by reviewing your financial position, credit history and business performance to identify areas for improvement.

What documents do I need for a loan application?

Typically you'll need a business plan, financial statements, bank statements, identification documents and business registration certificates. We help you prepare these.

What types of loans are available for small businesses?

Options include bank loans, microfinance loans, SACCO loans and alternative financing from fintech lenders. We help you find the right fit for your needs.

How can you help me get a loan?

We help you prepare your business, develop strong applications, structure your financing and identify the right lenders for your needs.

Do you guarantee loan approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval, we significantly improve your chances by ensuring you're well-prepared and presenting a compelling case to lenders.
