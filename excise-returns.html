Overview

What is Excise Returns Filing?

Excise duty returns filing is a monthly obligation for businesses dealing with excisable goods and services including alcohol, tobacco products, betting, gaming, motor vehicles and other specified products. Returns must be filed by the 20th of every month.

FAQ

Common questions about excise returns.

When is the excise returns deadline?

Excise returns must be filed by the 20th of every month following the transaction month.

What products are subject to excise duty?

Products include alcoholic beverages, tobacco products, betting and gaming, motor vehicles, and other specified goods and services.

What happens if I don't file excise returns?

Failure to file excise returns can result in penalties, interest charges, and potential legal consequences including business closure.

Do I need an excise license?

Yes, businesses dealing in excisable goods require an excise license from KRA. We can help you obtain and maintain your license.

Can you help with excise compliance?
