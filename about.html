About Makivan

A practical advisory firm for businesses ready to operate with discipline.

Founded in 2022 in Nairobi, Makivan Consultants serves private companies, small businesses and industrial sectors across Kenya and Uganda.

Our journey

We started with one goal: help businesses solve real operational problems.

Makivan Consultants was created to give business owners access to professional, tailored advice across tax, finance, HR, registration, compliance and growth. Our work is designed for leaders who need trusted support and clear execution, not vague theory.

Mission

To provide management consulting services that help organizations improve performance, operate efficiently, solve business challenges and meet their goals.

Vision

To become a driving force for business knowledge, professional consulting and enterprise development at national and international level.
