FAQ

Common questions about returns filing.

When are tax returns due in Kenya?

VAT, PAYE, withholding tax and excise returns are due by the 20th of the following month. Income tax returns for individuals and companies are due by June 30th each year.

What happens if I file my returns late?

Late filing attracts penalties and interest charges from KRA. We help ensure timely filing to avoid these costs and maintain good compliance standing.

Do you handle both individuals and companies?

Yes. We provide returns filing services for individuals, companies, partnerships and other business entities across all tax types.

What documents do I need for returns filing?

Typically you'll need sales and purchase records, employee payroll details, bank statements and previous tax returns. We'll guide you on specific requirements.

Can you help with past returns and penalties?

Yes. We can help you review past returns, correct errors and negotiate penalty waivers or payment plans with KRA where applicable.
