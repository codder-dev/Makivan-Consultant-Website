Overview

What is Turnover Tax?

Turnover tax is a simplified tax regime for small businesses in Kenya with annual turnover of KES 5 million or less. It's calculated at 3% of gross sales and filed monthly, making it easier for small businesses to stay compliant with KRA.

FAQ

Common questions about turnover tax.

What is the turnover tax rate?

Turnover tax is calculated at 3% of your monthly gross sales for businesses with annual turnover below KES 5 million.

When is the turnover tax deadline?

Turnover tax returns must be filed by the 20th of every month following the transaction month.

What happens if my business exceeds KES 5 million?

If your annual turnover exceeds KES 5 million, you'll need to transition from turnover tax to VAT and income tax. We can help with this transition.

Do I need to register for turnover tax?

Yes, you need to register for turnover tax with KRA. We can help you with the registration process.

Can you help with turnover tax compliance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support for turnover tax registration, filing, and compliance with KRA requirements.
