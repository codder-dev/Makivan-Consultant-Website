Questions clients ask

Quick answers before we talk.

Can Makivan help with KRA and iTax issues?

Yes. We support returns filing, iTax analysis, compliance certificates, KRA PIN applications and related tax administration.

Do you register companies in Kenya?

Yes. We assist with business names, limited liability companies, LLPs, director changes, beneficial ownership and annual returns.

What documents should I prepare before contacting you?

For tax matters, prepare your KRA PIN, iTax access details and recent notices or returns. For registration, prepare identification details, proposed business names and ownership information.

Do you work with clients outside Nairobi?

Yes. Makivan serves Nairobi, Ongata Rongai, wider Kenya, Uganda and remote clients across East Africa.

Can I start through WhatsApp?

Yes. Use the live chat button or call directly, and we will guide you to the right next step.
