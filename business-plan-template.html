FAQ

Common questions about business plans.

Why do I need a business plan?

A business plan helps you clarify your business goals, attract investors, secure funding, and guide your decision-making. It's essential for both startups and established businesses.

What should I include in my business plan?

Your business plan should include executive summary, company description, products/services, market analysis, competitive analysis, marketing strategy, financial projections, and management team information.

How long should a business plan be?

Most business plans are between 20-30 pages, but the length depends on your business complexity. Our template covers all key areas.

Can I get professional help with my business plan?

Yes, Makivan Consultants provides professional business plan writing, review, and consulting services. Contact us for expert guidance.

How often should I update my business plan?

You should review and update your business plan at least annually or whenever there are significant changes in your business or market conditions.
