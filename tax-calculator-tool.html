FAQ

Common questions about tax calculations.

What are the current PAYE tax bands?

10% on first 24,000; 25% on next 8,333; 30% on next 467,667; 32.5% on next 300,000; 35% on amounts above 800,000.

What is the VAT rate in Kenya?

The standard VAT rate in Kenya is 16% for most goods and services.

What is Turnover Tax?

Turnover Tax is a simplified tax for small businesses with annual turnover below KES 5 million. It is calculated at 3% of gross sales.

How is Rental Income Tax calculated?

Rental Income Tax is 10% of the net rental income after deducting allowable expenses.

What is the Housing Levy rate?

The Housing Levy is 1.5% of gross salary, matched by an equal employer contribution.
