FAQ

Common questions about tax calculations.

What are the current PAYE tax bands?

10% on first 24,000; 25% on next 8,333; 30% on next 467,667; 32.5% on next 300,000; 35% on amounts above 800,000.

How is NSSF calculated for employees?

Employee NSSF is 6% of pensionable pay up to the Tier 1 limit (2026: 9,000). This means max employee NSSF is KES 540.

How is SHIF calculated?

For income below 6,000: KES 300. For income 6,000 and above: 2.75% of gross salary.

What is the Housing Levy rate?

The Housing Levy is 1.5% of gross salary, matched by an equal employer contribution.

What is the personal relief amount?

Personal relief is KES 2,400 per month (KES 28,800 per year) deducted from your PAYE.
