FAQ

Common questions about tax compliance.

What is a tax compliance certificate?

A tax compliance certificate is issued by KRA confirming that your business has met all its tax obligations. It's required for tenders, contracts and various business transactions.

How do I get a tax compliance certificate?

You must have filed all your tax returns and paid all outstanding taxes. We help you meet these requirements and obtain your certificate.

What is SHA compliance?

SHA (Social Health Authority) compliance involves registering employees for health insurance and making monthly contributions.

What about NSSF compliance?

NSSF compliance involves registering employees for pension contributions and filing monthly returns.

Can you help with past compliance issues?

Yes, we help resolve past compliance issues, negotiate with KRA and get your business back on track.
